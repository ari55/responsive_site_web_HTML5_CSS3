YAS-875EX

Description

The new Yamaha YAS-875EX Custom EX alto saxophone marks a huge step forward for saxophone design. Offering players a wealth of new musical possibilities, the new Custom EX has been designed in collaboration with some of the world’s most talented saxophonists, including Nobuya Sugawa, Jean-Yves Fourmeau and Otis Murphy. Their invaluable input has meant that Yamaha have been able to create an instrument that sounds great, is comfortable to play, and that constantly evolves with the player.

Commentaire

(Note: all pictures are after one year) I bought this pan exactly one year ago. It has converted me to Cast Iron after growing up with rusty pans, always thought Teflon was superior, Nope, Nada, Zip! Sure there is a little rust on the bottom of the pan but this is just me being lazy. The cooking surface is flawless. I use this pan 2-3 times a week, It actually has gotten better over time. Besides cooking eggs, use this for just about everything else on the stove top. I'll even do a steak when I don't feel like using the BBQ. It is heavy, I'm a grown man, holding it with one hand is limited to about 10 seconds.

Good product. Very robust construction, the thing weighs a lot! You will definitely need two hands to transfer this from the stove to the oven..

Pan is extremely heavy. I'm not sure if I received a defective unit, but the pan I received was definitely not preseasoned. It is impossible for me to cook anything except meats on this pan without sticking.
